About Me

Jose Ricardo Garduno is a handyman who initially started 20 years ago working on a construction site and doing small jobs around town. Growing up, Mr. Garduno had to fix everything around him. This led him into remodeling. He remodeled not only inside a house, but outside of one too. In 2017 Mr. Garduno realized he could turn his passion into a business, with the goals of making clients happy, and spending more time with his family.
